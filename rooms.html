INSTRUCTIONS TO BE FOLLOWED BY THE STUDENTS
STAYING IN THE HALL

1. A parent must be brought to the meeting with the provost before going up to the hall.

2. A copy of all charge’s payment voucher (hall office copy) must be submitted to the hall office before entering the hall.

3. The student must be accompanied by a parent when coming up. Otherwise, the student will not be allowed to take the seat.
4. No furniture items can be brought while boarding in hall.

5. Any untoward incident should be reported to the authorities immediately.

6. Various expensive items such as television, fridge, audio set, jewelry, expensive watches, musical instruments, microwave, rice cooker etc. cannot be brought or kept inside the hall.

7. Various dangerous goods, such as any prohibited goods, explosives/ explosives, weapons, Irons, heaters, stoves, etc. cannot be brought or kept.

8. Large sums of money cannot be brought or kept.

9. Students will keep their money and other belongings under their own responsibility.

10. No smoking is allowed inside the hall.

11. Intoxicating or keeping any intoxicating substance inside the hall is prohibited.

12. Food bills must be paid by 10th of every month. Otherwise, food is off from 11th.

13. Hall charges are to be paid by 5th of every month.

13. In any circumstances charges cannot be kept due.

14. If the seat rent or food bill is due, the authority can stop the food at any time of the month on the 1st notice or can cancel the seat.

15. Female students will not be allowed to meet in the hall area with anyone other than authorized persons.

16. Students cannot share any food items nor exchange any gifts with anyone other than parents or authorized persons in the premises.

17. When going out of the room, students should lock their room at their own responsibility.

18. Girls must enter the hall by 9.30 pm and male students by 10.00 pm. If you are unable to enter within the specific time, you must enter the hall subject to the permission of the authorities.

19. Perishable food cannot be stored inside the hall.

20. Cooking cannot be done under own management.

21. Treat everyone with kindness.

22. Seat will be canceled in case of employment outside (part time/full time).

23. No name or any kind of writing can be done on the hall’s materials.

24. Any damage to government property will be held responsible for the student concerned.

25. Any kind of party, assembly, organization and any political activity shall not be allowed inside the hall.

26. No load shall be carried by the hall staff.

27. The cleaners will not work in the room in the absence of students.

28. No transaction can be done with the staff.

29. Entering or exiting the hall must be duly recorded in the designated registrar.
